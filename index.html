Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if therapy is right for me?

Therapy can benefit anyone seeking personal growth, dealing with life challenges, or experiencing emotional distress. Our free consultation can help you determine if our services match your needs.

What should I expect in my first session?

Your first session is about getting to know you and understanding your goals. We'll discuss your history, current concerns, and develop a treatment plan tailored to your needs.

Do you accept insurance?

We work with most major insurance providers. Our team can help verify your benefits and explain your coverage options during your consultation.

Is online therapy as effective as in-person?

Research shows that online therapy can be just as effective as in-person sessions for many conditions. We use secure, HIPAA-compliant platforms to ensure your privacy.
